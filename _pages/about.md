My research uses computational models to describe the ways in which humans solve complex problems together. For instance, how do we choose between many possible representations for the difficult problem? How can we communicate these representations to impart knowledge or work better together?

I am a PhD student, working with [Professor Mark Ho](https://markkho.github.io) in the [Computation & Decision-Making Lab](https://codec-lab.github.io). We are part of the Department of Psychology at [New York University](https://as.nyu.edu/departments/psychology.html). Prior to my PhD, I was a lab manager for the [Computational Cognitive Science Lab](https://cocosci.princeton.edu) & the [Concepts and Cognition Lab](https://cognition.princeton.edu) at [Princeton University](https://psych.princeton.edu).
